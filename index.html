about me

I'm an editor by education (after Polish philology at the University of Wrocław), I also completed postgraduate studies in typography (AKT in Warsaw).

In 2017 I started to learn coding on my own and for almost 2 years I was implementing websites. At that time I became interested in users experiences. Because I also love reading and learning psychology and sociology, I'm very excited about human behaviour and I like to ask "why?", I realized that everything I know, what interests me and what I would like to learn, fits beautifully to move towards UX. So I did.

Now I am employed by a well-known advertising agency, where I work for clients such as Microsoft and P&G. I also work as a proofreader in a magazine about designing digital products. Now my main aim is to engage myself more in research, as something that I consider as my absolute dream job, that will maybe make me more truly useful for myself and for others.

Privately I'm interested in speedway, criminal stories, strolling and engaging talks (I like to mix the last two). I collect books with nice covers. I have a very special place in my heart for animals (all animals in general, but most of all I love dogs, especially Pekingese).
