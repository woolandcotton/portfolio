about me

I'm an editor by education (after Polish philology at the University of Wrocław), I also completed postgraduate studies in typography (AKT in Warsaw). In 2017 I started to learn coding on my own and for almost 2 years I was implementing websites. At that time I became interested in users experiences. Because I love reading and learning psychology and sociology and I'm very excited about human behaviour, I realized that everything I know, what interests me and what I would like to learn, fits beautifully to move towards UX. I am employed by a well-known advertising agency, where I work for clients such as Microsoft and P&G.

Privately I'm interested in speedway, eating potatoes in every form and strolling everywhere I am. I collect books with nice covers. Animals have a special place in my heart (all animals, but most of all I love dogs, especially Pekingese).
